About Me

Hiiiiiii, all! My name is Trang Do.

I am an Austin native and I graduated from the University of Texas at Austin. I majored in International Relations and Global Studies with specializations in Global Business and Asian Studies: Korean. I am Vietnamese-American and a first generation college graduate.

I'm an artistic person. I enjoy drawing, painting, photography, and video editing. In my free time, I also like to read books and check out new music. After a long day of productivity, my favorite way to end the day is by winning a couple games of League before bed.

Aside from a nice job, what I look forward to most after this bootcamp is to be able to design super cool websites with ease. I love that web design serves as a creative outlet for my artistic passions and I hope to be an amazing web developer one day.
